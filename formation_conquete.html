Conquest Calculator Formation Guide

  1. To correctly fill in the calculator in the Technology section, you must determine the teams’ technologies.
  2. You must determine whether the team has Total Troop Attack +1 or Total Troop Defense +1 technology.
  1. To determine the opponent’s +1 Troop Attack tech, they must attack you.
  2. To determine +1 Troop Defense tech, you must be the attacker.
  1. Left column (attack): Engaged troops 2500, Total attack value 2500. Since both numbers are identical, there is no tech.
  2. Right column (defense): Engaged troops 2000, Total troop defense value 2000. Since both numbers are identical, there is no tech.
  1. Example below to determine total troop attack value.
  2. If the total troop attack value is double the engaged troops, it means +1 Troop Attack tech is active.
  1. Example below to determine the presence of +1 Troop Defense tech.
  2. If the total troop defense value is double the engaged troops, the tech is active.

A. ACCESSING WATCHTOWER STATS

The watchtower defense modifier increases tower resistance by +20%.

B. Without Watchtower Modifier Technology

The first image shows a tower without the modifier tech. The default value of a level 1 watchtower is 1.20.

C. With Watchtower Modifier Technology

Second example with +20% Watchtower Defense Modifier tech. Add 20% to the default value of 1.20.

Example calculation:

1.20 + (20% + 20%) = 1.68

From this you can also deduce the technology path chosen.

These technologies have minimal impact on war outcomes but help identify the opponent’s tech path.

The default total hero attack or defense value is 75.

If you divide total hero value by engaged troops and get 80, it means +5 Hero tech is active. It is also possible to find 90 if two tech upgrades are active.

Example:
  • Engaged troops: 1
  • Total hero value: 80
  • 80 ÷ 1 = 80 → +5 Hero tech
  • This applies to both hero attack and defense.

This helps determine technology choices and plan strategy.
